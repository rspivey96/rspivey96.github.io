Projects
CommBo - Digital Communication Device Spring 2018

 For every 100,000 people, there are 536 speech-impaired individuals who can benefit from the use of Augmentative and Alternative Communication (AAC) devices. AAC devices currently available are either difficult to adjust or expensive to own. We aim to remedy this with CommBo, a web-based, speech-generating picture communication board that alleviates the major difficulties commonly experienced by AAC users. CommBo offers customizability far beyond that of a physical picture board and leverages machine learning to provide intelligent suggestions. This enables heightened communication that can be brought to any internet-connected device. CommBo is designed for those patients suffering from stroke induced aphasia, autistic spectrum disorder, or any trauma that impacts the person’s ability to communicate effectively.
